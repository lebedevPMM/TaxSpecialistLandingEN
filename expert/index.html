About the Expert

Vasile Lupu

International tax and investment lawyer with over 15 years of experience advising private investors, entrepreneurs and family offices on complex cross-border asset structures and regulatory matters.

Core Specialization

Tax and corporate advisory focused on restoring operational and legal control over international asset structures. Direct representation of clients before tax authorities, financial institutions and regulators across multiple jurisdictions, including obtaining approvals required for the sale and transfer of securities into the Russian jurisdiction. Advisory on removal of asset segregation on Type "C" accounts.

Track Record

Advised on the unblocking of assets and restoration of control over international corporate structures exceeding RUB 5 billion. Successfully represented Russian investors in restoring control over foreign legal entities, bank accounts and securities across Europe and other jurisdictions.

Extensive experience obtaining individual licenses from Euroclear, Clearstream and OFAC required for securities transfers and transactions. Regular interaction with the NSD and the Government Commission of the Russian Federation within the framework of Presidential Decrees No. 95, 138, 198, 520, 844 and related regulations.

Expertise

International tax planning and cross-border structuring, CFC compliance and reporting, 45+ corporate restructurings across multiple jurisdictions, participation in M&A transactions involving assets exceeding USD 400 million, structuring international payments between Russia and the EU under current regulatory constraints including KYC verification standards, AML compliance, automatic exchange of information and international tax reporting requirements.

Operating Model

Each engagement is supported by a coordinated network of local advisors — tax professionals, lawyers, banking specialists, compliance experts and international partners. The approach focuses on practical execution, risk management and measurable outcomes, delivering integrated turnkey solutions for capital protection and cross-border operations.

Professional Background

Vasile began his legal career at Lomonosov Moscow State University and in international consulting firms of the Big Four, later leading tax practices within Russian corporate groups. He currently advises several family offices with assets and business interests across Russia, the EU, the BVI and the UAE.
