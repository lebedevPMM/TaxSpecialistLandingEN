Frequently Asked Questions

What types of assets can you help unblock?

Securities held in Euroclear, Clearstream and NSD accounts, funds on Type "C" accounts, shares in foreign entities, bank deposits and other financial instruments subject to sanctions-related restrictions.

Which jurisdictions do you cover?

Russia, Cyprus, Belgium, Luxembourg, France, Switzerland, the United Kingdom, Spain, Turkey, Romania, Latvia, Moldova, BVI, Panama, Seychelles and the UAE. The network of local partners extends to additional jurisdictions as needed.

How long does the licensing process typically take?

Timelines vary by jurisdiction and the nature of the assets. Individual licenses from Euroclear or Clearstream usually take 3 to 9 months. We provide a realistic timeline estimate after reviewing your specific situation.

Is the initial consultation confidential?

Yes. All communications are subject to attorney-client privilege from the first contact. No information is shared with third parties without explicit written consent.

What information do I need to provide for an initial assessment?

A general description of the asset structure, the jurisdictions involved, and the nature of the issue you are facing. Specific documents can be discussed during the first consultation.

Do you work with corporate clients or only individuals?

Both. We advise private investors, entrepreneurs, family offices and corporate groups on cross-border structuring, tax optimization and regulatory matters.
